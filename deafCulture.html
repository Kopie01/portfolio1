Deaf Culture Booklet

This is a booklet I designed exploring Deaf Culture. This involved alot of research and I also took a basic course in New Zealand Sign Language to get more insight in the lives of deaf people. I used illustration to simulate the sign movement that is such a large aspect of deaf communication. Many of the illustrations are actual signs used in New Zealand Sign Language. I used bold colours to represent the visual part of the culture and I wanted it to appear friendly, fun and approachable to non-deaf people. My use of type for this project often uses the letterforms to express movement, as writing is the visual communication that both deaf and non-deaf people can understand, so it is a connecting element.
