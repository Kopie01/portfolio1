About Me

Hi there!

Welcome to my portfolio site.
As I am currently studying a diploma in UX and web design, this site is a work in progress. I have included some of my recent web projects but there will be more professional digital work added as I learn more in the next 6 months. This site itself is designed and coded by me.
I also hold a Bachelor of Design in Visual Communication. After finishing that qualification in 2009 I was employed for 2 years as an in-house designer for a publishing company working on a range of magazines covering various topics such as health, education, youth entertainment and education (Tearaway magazine) and business.
I then took some time to work and travel in the UK and Europe which opened my eyes to many different styles of work and has also taught me what makes New Zealand unique as a country. After returning from my travels I became interested in interactive design and how, combined with graphic design, it can create a beautiful but functional experience for the user. This lead me to begin my diploma. I am inspired by different cultures, interesting type, architecture, history and the natural world. I especially love illustration and how it can be used to create a tactile feel within the digital world.
I hope you enjoy browsing my portfolio.
